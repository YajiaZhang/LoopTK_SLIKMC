\subsection{Detailed Description}
Encapsulates a single atom in the protein loop. \doxyref{PAtom}{p.}{classPAtom} contains methods to translate and apply matrix transformations to the atom's position; find other atom(s) in collision with it; and perform traversals of the graph represented by atoms and their bonds. Makes an assumption that the atoms of the same type are the same size.
