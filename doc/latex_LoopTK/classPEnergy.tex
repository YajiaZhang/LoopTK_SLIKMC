\subsection{Detailed Description}
{\tt \doxyref{PEnergy}{p.}{classPEnergy}} contains methods to calculate the free energy for pairs of atoms and proteins.

Calculates the collision energy between atoms {\tt a1} and {\tt a2}. If the radius of {\tt a1} and {\tt a2} are r1 and r2 respectively, the collision energy is 1/d$^\wedge$2 - 1/d0$^\wedge$2, where d is the distance between two atom centers, and d0=COLLISION\_\-THRESHOLD$\ast$(r1+r2). The COLLISION\_\-THRESHOLD is set to 0.75 in \doxyref{PConstants.h}{p.}{PConstants_8h}.
