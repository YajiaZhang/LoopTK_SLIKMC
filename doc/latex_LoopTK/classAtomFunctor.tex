\subsection{Detailed Description}
A functor to be called on an atom and its source bond ({\em not\/} destination bond) during a graph traversal of atoms and bonds. Create a subclass of {\tt \doxyref{Atom\-Functor}{p.}{classAtomFunctor}} to specify custom behavior.
